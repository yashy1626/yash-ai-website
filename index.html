Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your cookies stay fresh? +

Our cookies stay fresh for up to 10 days when stored in an airtight container at room temperature. For extended freshness, you can freeze them for up to 3 months.

Do you offer gluten-free options? +

Yes! We offer a selection of gluten-free cookies made with premium gluten-free flour. Please mention your preference when ordering.

What's your shipping policy? +

We ship within 24 hours of order confirmation. Standard shipping takes 2-3 business days. Orders over $50 qualify for free shipping.

Can I customize my order? +

Absolutely! We offer custom orders for corporate gifts, weddings, and special events. Please contact us for details on minimum orders and pricing.

Do you offer returns or refunds? +

If you're not completely satisfied, we offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase. We also replace damaged items at no extra cost.

Are your ingredients organic? +

Yes, we use 100% organic, non-GMO ingredients. All our suppliers are certified and regularly audited for quality and safety.
